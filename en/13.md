# 13. If ChatGPT Isn't an Option

If, for any reason, you find yourself locked out of ChatGPT, don't sweat it. You can dive into open-source AI models that you can run locally, for example, LM Studio.

LM Studio is your go-to local platform for running generative AI models, geared perfectly for the everyday user. Picture this: you're able to run open-source large language models, like GPT, right on your own laptop without needing to be a tech whiz or having pricey servers. Talk about user-friendly—its interface is as straightforward as it gets. Just download and install, and you're ready to rock offline with text generation, Q&A, and more. It's like having your own tech lab at your fingertips. Check it out at their official site: [LM Studio](https://lmstudio.ai/).
